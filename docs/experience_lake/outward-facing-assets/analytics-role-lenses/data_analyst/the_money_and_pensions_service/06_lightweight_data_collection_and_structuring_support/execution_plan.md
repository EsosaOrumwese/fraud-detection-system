- why does the slice stop at a lightweight mechanism rather than a stronger collection-platform claim?

Allowed mechanism posture:
- one bounded structuring layer
- one standardised capture surface
- one compact reusable mechanism supporting downstream analytical work

Disallowed mechanism posture:

- importing generic systems language as fake mechanism proof

Decision rule:

  - surface standardisation
  - downstream analytical use
