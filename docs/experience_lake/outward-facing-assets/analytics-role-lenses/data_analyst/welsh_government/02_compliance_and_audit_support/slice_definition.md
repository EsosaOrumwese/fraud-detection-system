## 1. Requirement Being Targeted

The requirement being targeted here is the Welsh Government `Payroll Data Analyst` expectation around:
- supporting internal or external audit with reliable data and analysis
- keeping outputs defensible in a rules-bound environment
- working inside compliance-sensitive operational conditions
- ensuring recurring analytical outputs can stand up to scrutiny

This maps directly to the candidate-profile requirement that the analyst should be able to sound like someone who:
- understands that operational analytics can sit inside audit-sensitive and statutory contexts
- can produce outputs that are traceable and review-ready
- can support audit or compliance review with controlled evidence
- can work within rules without overstating ownership of the rules themselves

This note exists because that requirement is easy to flatten into the vague statement:
`good with compliance`

That is not the real burden.

The employer is asking for something sharper:
- defensible outputs
- traceable rules and evidence
- review-ready support for audit or compliance scrutiny
- recurring control discipline rather than ad hoc checking

The current platform does not contain a literal payroll-compliance office, `HMRC` interaction layer, or live statutory reporting function, so the slice has to use an honest analogue.

The closest truthful analogue is:
- one recurring validation-and-reconciliation control pack already established in Welsh Government `A + D`
- one explicit rule-authority and discrepancy posture
- one audit-ready summary showing what was checked, what failed, and why the released view is defensible

So for this slice:
- `support internal and external audits` maps to `produce one compact audit-support pack over the recurring control lane`

- `maintain records and outputs that stand up to scrutiny` maps to `create one traceability-ready output family plus one defensibility note`

The claim must therefore remain:
- compliance-support shaped
- audit-support shaped
- traceability shaped
- platform-truth bounded
- careful not to imply literal statutory authority, payroll-law ownership, or a full audit function where this slice only proves one bounded review-ready control pack

Default first-pass posture:
- defensibility first
- traceability second
- audit-support and review-readiness first
- stronger compliance-language only where the bounded evidence genuinely supports it
