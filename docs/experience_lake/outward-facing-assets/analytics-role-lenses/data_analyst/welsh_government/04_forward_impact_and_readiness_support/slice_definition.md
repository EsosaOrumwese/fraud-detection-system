## 1. Requirement Being Targeted

The requirement being targeted here is the Welsh Government `Payroll Data Analyst` expectation around:
- anticipating the impact of expected change on recurring payroll-style operational outputs
- supporting forward-looking review or readiness when recurring conditions suggest future pressure
- helping operational teams prepare for impact rather than only reacting after release
- using evidence to frame what should be watched, checked, or protected next

This maps directly to the candidate-profile requirement that the analyst should be able to sound like someone who:
- can think beyond the current cycle
- can use recurring evidence to anticipate where future effort or risk may surface
- can support readiness planning or scenario review in a rules-bound operational setting
- can do that without overstating formal forecasting or statutory modelling ownership

This note exists because that responsibility is easy to overstate as:
`I forecast payroll impacts`

That is not the truthful platform burden here.

The employer is asking for something sharper:
- recognise when a recurring condition has forward-looking implications
- frame the likely impact in a bounded and decision-useful way
- support readiness or scenario review before the next cycle
- do so without pretending to own a full legislative or payroll-forecasting estate
