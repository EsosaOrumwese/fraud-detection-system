## 1. Requirement Being Targeted

The requirement being targeted here is the Hertfordshire Partnership University NHS Foundation Trust `Data and Information Officer` expectation around:
- monitoring performance against service or Trust targets
- reporting target performance to managers or service leads
- helping teams understand shortfalls
- helping teams act on performance information rather than only receive it
- supporting the management and achievement of waiting-time targets and broader `KPI` delivery
- identifying risks to delivery and supporting remedial action

This maps directly to the Hertfordshire candidate-profile requirement that the analyst should be able to sound like someone who:
- can work in a target-driven service environment
- can help teams monitor performance and understand shortfalls
- can track `KPIs` and waiting-time style delivery pressure
- can support focused remediation rather than just passive reporting

This note exists because that requirement is easy to flatten into the vague statement:
`good at performance reporting`

That is not the real burden.

The employer is asking for something sharper:
- target-performance monitoring
- shortfall visibility
- `KPI` and waiting-time style management support
- practical remediation support

## 2. The Analogue On The Current Platform

The current platform does not contain literal Adult Community Mental Health services, live `NHS` waiting-time pathways, or a genuine clinical performance regime, so the slice has to use an honest analogue.

The closest truthful analogue is:
- one governed monthly service-performance lane
- one target-style performance pack over that lane
- one explicit shortfall or underperforming focus pocket
- one bounded remediation-support interpretation tied to that shortfall

So for this slice:
- `monitor performance against targets` maps to `track one stable performance lane against an explicit expected threshold or reference level`
- `support waiting-time targets and broader KPI management` maps to `show one target-style service-performance reading with a visible underperforming pocket`
- `support remedial action` maps to `package one bounded follow-up recommendation or remediation-support note tied to the shortfall`

Important platform-boundary clarification:
- the target or expected level in this slice does not need to be a literal `NHS` access target copied from the job advert
- it can be an explicit platform-side reference level such as:
  - governed overall baseline
  - peer-band average
  - stable prior-window level
- the key requirement is that the reference is explicit, disciplined, and good enough to separate acceptable performance from a real shortfall pocket

The claim must therefore remain:
- target-performance shaped
- service-pressure shaped
- platform-truth bounded
- careful not to imply real `NHS` clinical target ownership or service-delivery ownership where this slice only proves one bounded performance-monitoring and remediation-support lane
