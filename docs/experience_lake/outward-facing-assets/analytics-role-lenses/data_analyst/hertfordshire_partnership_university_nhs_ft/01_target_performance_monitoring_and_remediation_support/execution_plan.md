- which inherited monthly lane is strongest for a target-style reading?
- what explicit reference level can act honestly as the target-style threshold?
- which pocket or band most clearly represents underperformance or delivery risk?
- can the remediation-support reading be stated from that same lane without widening scope?
- do the row counts, fields, and current notes show that the slice can remain compact?

Required checks:
- continuity of the inherited monthly performance lane
- continuity of the supporting detail cut

Those checks should be implemented as:
