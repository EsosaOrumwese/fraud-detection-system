## 1. Requirement Being Targeted

The requirement being targeted here is the Hertfordshire Partnership University NHS Foundation Trust `Data and Information Officer` expectation around:
- working across services to improve data quality
- understanding how required activity capture and accurate entry affect whether information is correct
- ensuring target-performance reporting remains valid
- helping services improve the quality of what gets recorded rather than treating data quality as a separate back-office issue

This maps directly to the Hertfordshire candidate-profile requirement that the analyst should be able to sound like someone who:
- understands that target performance depends on valid activity capture
- understands that accurate entry underpins trustworthy reporting
- can identify weak points in recording or capture logic
- can support practical improvement in the quality of what is recorded

This note exists because that requirement is easy to flatten into the vague statement:
`good at data quality`

That is not the real burden.

The employer is asking for something sharper:
- data quality in service of performance validity
- clear linkage between recorded activity and target interpretation
- one practical improvement posture tied to the service lane

## 2. The Analogue On The Current Platform

The current platform does not contain literal Adult Mental Health activity-entry workflows, clinical recording teams, or live service-entry controls, so the slice has to use an honest analogue.

The closest truthful analogue is:
- one governed target-performance lane already established through Hertfordshire `A + D`
- one explicit validity-sensitive weak point in that lane
- one demonstration of how the weak point affects the reliability of the performance reading
- one bounded improvement or control recommendation that protects the lane

So for this slice:
- `improve data quality within services` maps to `identify one weak point in the governed performance-validity chain and support a practical correction`
- `understand required activities and accurate entry to ensure information is correct` maps to `show that the target-performance reading depends on one explicit validity or control condition in the analytical lane`
- `work with services to improve that` maps to `package one bounded recommendation that improves the reliability of what the lane is relying on`

The claim must therefore remain:
- performance-validity shaped
- service-recording shaped in analogue rather than literal service-entry ownership
- platform-truth bounded
- careful not to imply literal clinical recording-policy ownership or whole-service data-quality ownership where this slice only proves one bounded validity issue and one improvement support action
