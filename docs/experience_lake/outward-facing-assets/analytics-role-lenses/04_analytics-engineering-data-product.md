## 1. What This Lens Means

`Analytics Engineering and Analytical Data Product` means reading the platform as a governed world that needs analytical outputs to be:
- shaped
- structured
- reusable
- stable
- easy for downstream consumers to work with

This lens is not:
- infrastructure for infrastructure's sake
- broad platform engineering
- raw ingestion or runtime ownership

It is about:
- turning governed surfaces into analytical tables, views, and semantic structures
- building reporting-ready and modelling-ready products
- preserving meaning while making data easier to consume

## 2. Why This Lens Fits This Platform World

The platform already has:
- governed truth surfaces
- linked behavioural and outcome data
- case and timeline structure
- multiple analytical use cases

That means there is real responsibility in shaping this world into:
- KPI-ready views
- model-ready slices
- cohort tables
- reporting-friendly outputs
- reusable analytical layers

## 4. What This Person Would Actually Do

Under this lens, the person would:
- shape governed data into reusable analytical tables and views
- create stable products for KPI, reporting, cohort, and model use
- define analytical grains and join paths suitable for downstream consumers
- preserve semantic meaning while reducing downstream friction
- package analytical data products for reporting, modelling, and decision-support use

## 5. What This Lens Would Naturally Produce

This lens would naturally produce:
- reusable SQL views
- KPI-ready analytical tables
- model-ready slices
- cohort and segment tables
- reporting-friendly dimensional outputs
- semantic-ready analytical layers

## 6. Practical Tooling Expression

This lens would naturally be expressed through:
- `SQL`
- notebook-based shaping where needed
- BI semantic preparation
- controlled extract and table design
- lineage and documentation support

## 7. Essence Of The Lens

`Analytics Engineering and Analytical Data Product` turns the platform into a source of reusable analytical products, and the person working through this lens becomes the one who makes governed data easier and safer for downstream analytical consumers to use.
