* predictive and statistical modelling

* data modelling, statistics, and machine learning

* SQL and Python

Your Midlands resume already captures this well, and your evidence slices back it up:

This is why the statement should lean heavily into **technical core + governed delivery**.

## 4. Where is your fit adjacent rather than direct?

This is where honesty matters.

Your adjacent, not-literal fit areas are:

* direct use of NHS datasets / Data Dictionary in practice
